# 🎯 FocusTrack

> Your ultimate, all-in-one productivity and lifestyle companion.

FocusTrack is a full-stack web application designed to help you build better habits, stay productive, and track your fitness goals. Whether you are managing daily tasks, utilizing the Pomodoro technique to study, or logging your daily workouts and diet, FocusTrack gamifies your growth with streaks, points, and leaderboards.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* ✅ **Task Management:** Organize your daily to-dos and stay on top of your schedule.
* 🍅 **Pomodoro Timer:** Boost your focus with a built-in, customizable Pomodoro timer.
* 💪 **Workout Tracking:** Log exercises by reps or time. Includes a live exercise timer and automatically calculates points based on your effort.
* 🥗 **Diet & Nutrition:** Keep track of your daily meals and nutritional goals.
* 🔥 **Streaks & Habits:** Build consistency! Watch your streak grow as you complete your daily objectives.
* 🏆 **Challenges & Leaderboard:** Compete with friends or other users by earning points from your workouts and tasks.
* 🔐 **Secure Authentication:** Complete user registration and login system, including a dedicated Admin panel.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

**Frontend**
* [React](https://reactjs.org/) (via [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/))
* [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) - For rapid, modern, and responsive UI styling.
* [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/) - Beautiful and consistent iconography.

**Backend**
* [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/) & [Express.js](https://expressjs.com/) - Fast and scalable server architecture.
* [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/) & [Mongoose](https://mongoosejs.com/) - NoSQL database for flexible data storage.
* **JWT** - JSON Web Tokens for secure authentication.
